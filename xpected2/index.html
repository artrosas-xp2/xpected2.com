Executive Summary

Xpected2 does not chase engagements. Engagements are scheduled. The Firm's founder operates at ranges the industry considers impersonal and altitudes the industry considers unreasonable, under a doctrine refined across 472 billable hours: the flag will still be there in fifteen minutes. What distinguishes the founder is not urgency — it is the complete absence of it, deployed with precision.

Filed Statement — Matter of the Distance
"He is not absent. He is approximately 200 meters out, uphill, and he has been watching you for some time. The distance is not avoidance. The distance is the practice."

Most operatives close distance to resolve a dispute. The founder holds it. From elevation, every contested objective becomes a matter of patient review — surveyed, documented, and, where the record requires it, concluded at 826 meters. And when the practice must travel, it does not walk. It rides.

Signature Service — El Caballo

The Firm's most recognizable asset. The Traverser Mark 2 — El Caballo — is not a vehicle. It is a philosophy with a drivetrain: direct vehicular contact administered at speed, passengers delivered to objectives they did not ask to visit, and 384 combatants removed from the field astride. Opposing counsel's account is on the record: the jeep came out of nowhere. It did not.

Closing Argument

The Firm has retained operatives who take objectives. It has retained an operative who becomes them. The founder provides the complement: an operative who has seen every objective on every map — from a considerable distance, through a scope, at his leisure.

Proximity, in the founder's operational calculus, is a service other people offer. The practice offers certainty: the shot will arrive, the jeep will arrive, and neither will be early. They will be exactly on time.
