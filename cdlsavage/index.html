Executive Summary

Every firm has a rainmaker. CDLsavage is the Firm's. The team carry, by the numbers and by acclamation: 11,789 kills at the only K/D ratio the accountants describe as profitable — 1.63, career, sustained — built on 10,560 sighted kills against a few hundred fired from the hip, presumably out of politeness. When a match must be won rather than discussed, the file goes to him.

Filed Statement — Matter of the Flashlight
"Hagental Nightfall. Zero night vision. Every deployment, he clears the map with a flashlight — announcing his position to anyone curious. It should not work. It continues to work."

A certified rotary-wing aviator — nineteen aircraft removed from opposing inventories, several from inside another aircraft — who nonetheless prefers, on principle, to work on land. The wings are a capability. The ground is the practice. And the practice has noticed that when he moves, the Firm moves with him: RogueRubio has been observed leaving the objective, briefly, to hold his pocket, and the founder himself has been seen following at a respectful distance, throwing health packs at him. Unprompted. Repeatedly.

Signature Service — Little Bird
