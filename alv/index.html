Executive Summary

Al-V_ does not carry the squad. He holds it together. The Firm's self-proclaimed glue guy posts more assists than kills — 5,876 against 5,121 — a ratio the other operatives cannot claim and would not survive. His doctrine is published on his letterhead: he does his best. The record shows, with some regularity, that his best is enough.

Filed Statement — Matter of the Terminal
"Did you know when you capture a terminal you don't have to hold square?"
— Asked sincerely, mid-capture, while holding a full toolkit he also did not know he could use

The statement rewards study. For an undisclosed period, the associate captured every terminal manually, continuously, holding square — unaware the button could be released, unaware the tools in his hands did anything at all. He has, in effect, been operating with one less button than the rest of the lobby, and posting a 100% ranked podium rate anyway. The Firm declines to teach him the button. The results argue against it.
